Project Overview: Face Detection Attention Tracker
At its core, this project is a specialized computer vision system designed to monitor and quantify human engagement. By leveraging real-time facial analysis, the system determines not just "if" a person is present, but "how" they are interacting with a specific focal point (like a screen, a classroom, or a retail display).

Core Functionality
Face Detection & Tracking: Utilizing high-speed algorithms to identify and follow facial bounding boxes across frames.

Head Pose Estimation: Calculating pitch, yaw, and roll to determine the direction of the user's gaze.

Eye-Tracking Integration: (Optional, depending on your build) Analyzing iris movement to refine focus accuracy.

Attention Scoring: A custom logic layer that translates "time spent looking at target" into a percentage-based engagement metric.
